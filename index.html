The Process

From Day One to First Day on the Job

A clear, six-step journey — from your first consultation to your first paycheck in a new role.

01
Free Career Consultation

A 30-minute call with a career advisor to understand where you are now, where you want to go, and which program gets you there fastest.

02
Tailored Learning Plan

Your mentor helps you set clear milestones, choose your schedule format, and align the curriculum to your specific career target — not a generic syllabus.

03
Learn from Active Practitioners

Instructor-led sessions cover the tools and workflows used on the job today. Live Q&A, code reviews, and real feedback — not recorded videos.

04
Build a Portfolio Under Mentorship

Every project is reviewed by your mentor with the same lens as a professional code review or creative brief — so your portfolio tells a credible story to employers.

05
Earn Your Certification

Internationally recognized certification upon completion — plus prep support for relevant industry exams where applicable.

06
Career Placement Support

Resume refinement, interview coaching, and warm introductions to our employer partner network. Your placement advisor stays with you until you land.
